Taking Care of the Boys

Necessary Items:

  • Food/Medicine
    • Orijen Regional Red (1 cup each per day)
    • White Rice (1/2 cup per day for Muttly)
    • Dasuquin Supplement (1 per day for Rufus)
    • CBD Treats (1 per day for Muttly)
  • Bully Sticks
  • Toys
    • Peppa Pig
    • Muttly's Duck
    • Kong Classics
  • Muttly's Crate

Daily Schedule:

  1. Wake up around 7:00 am, take boys out first thing
  2. Breakfast when back in from the walk
  3. Big walk around 1:00 pm - they love to go to the dog park past the Lighthouse
  4. Dinner around 6:30pm
  5. Take them outside IMMEDIATELY after dinner, otherwise Muttly may have an accident
  6. Last walk before bedtime around 10:00pm

Other Things to Know:

  • Should go out every 2-4 hours (Muttly is prone to accidents)
  • They are usually very sleepy in the morning, more active and excitable in the afternoon
  • Bully sticks are a great distraction if they are acting needy
  • Muttly tries to lunge at cars and bikes - make sure to keep him on a short leash
  • When they play they make scary-sounding "gremlin" noises, it is not a cause for concern!
